THE PROBLEM

Why Current Smart Homes Fail in India

Most smart home solutions in India are built for always-on internet and cloud.

  • Stop functioning when the internet is unavailable.
  • Store user voice data on external cloud servers.
  • Are expensive and impractical for common households.
  • Do not support legacy electrical wiring.
  • Require monthly subscriptions and hidden cloud costs.

This makes them unreliable, unsafe, and unsuitable for real Indian conditions.

Lina™ was built to solve exactly this.

OUR SOLUTION

Local Processing. No Cloud. No Compromise.

Lina™ processes all voice commands locally inside the device and integrates with existing wiring.

🧠

🛡️

Private by Design

Drop-In Integration
